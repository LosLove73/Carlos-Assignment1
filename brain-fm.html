Tested on Brain and Behavior


Scientist at Brain.fm work with collaborators at acadmeic

institutions to run experiments.


We look at the affects of our technology on the brain, using

fRMI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavioral tests,

measuring performance on simple games.


Our experiments always include a control condition

('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm

technology applied, so we can be sure our tech is what makes

the difference.
